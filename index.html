A two-day journey away from my PC.

Blue Screen

A simple digital illustration, showcasing the start of my 48 hour journey without my PC.

This is Not a PC

While the scope of the media strike focused on staying away from my PC, I wanted to go the extra mile and also avoid applications that I would typically also use on my desktop, as well as, social media. With this illustration, I try to signify this by blurring the applications that were inaccessible to me during the 48 hours.

The Adversaries

A simple collage showcasing the applications I use daily, and avoided during the media strike.

Comfort in Chaos

With this artwork, I aim to show the chaos and noise that I often experience when using my PC, by warping the icons shown above. Despite the chaos and noise, there is a certain comfort I find in the familiarity of these digital tools.
